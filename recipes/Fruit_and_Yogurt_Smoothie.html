Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

The recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Blend the banana, yogurt, sugar, pineapple juice, strawberries, orange juice and milk in a blender until smooth.
